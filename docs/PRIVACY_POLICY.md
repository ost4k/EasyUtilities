# Privacy Policy for EasyUtilities

**Effective date:** March 15, 2026

EasyUtilities ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information EasyUtilities processes, how it is used, and what choices you have.

## 1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the EasyUtilities desktop application for Windows.

## 2. Data We Process

EasyUtilities is designed to work primarily on your device. The app does **not** require account registration and does **not** include in-app analytics, ads, or third-party tracking SDKs.

Depending on the features you use, the app may process:

- App settings (for example: language, theme, enabled toggles, selected feature modes).
- Temporary clipboard content (for example: OCR text output, optional exported file list text).
- User-selected files for backup/restore of settings and desktop icon layout.
- Desktop and shell-related configuration data needed to apply selected Windows utility features.

## 3. How Data Is Used

We process data only to provide app functionality you actively trigger, including:

- Saving and restoring your app preferences.
- Running utility features such as OCR from screen selection, file list export, and shell tweaks.
- Importing/exporting backups you explicitly choose.

## 4. Where Data Is Stored

- Data is stored locally on your Windows device.
- Backup/export files are stored only in the location you choose.
- We do not operate a cloud backend for EasyUtilities features described above.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell your personal data.

We do not share app-generated data with third parties, except:

- when required by law, legal process, or valid government request;
- if necessary to protect rights, safety, or prevent abuse;
- if you explicitly choose to share exported files yourself.

## 6. Network and Telemetry

EasyUtilities does not use built-in advertising identifiers, third-party analytics trackers, or profiling systems.

If platform components (for example Microsoft Store infrastructure or Windows OS services) process technical diagnostics independently, such processing is governed by their own terms and privacy policies.

## 7. Permissions and System Access

Some features require elevated Windows permissions (for example, administrator rights for certain shell modifications). These actions are initiated only when you enable and confirm them.

## 8. Data Retention

- Settings are retained locally until you change, reset, or remove the app.
- Files you export remain where you saved them until you delete them.
- Clipboard data is managed by your operating system and user actions.

## 9. Your Choices

You can at any time:

- disable individual features in the app;
- remove or replace exported backup files;
- uninstall EasyUtilities to stop all local processing by the app.

## 10. Children's Privacy

EasyUtilities is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## 11. International Users

Because processing is primarily local on your own device, you control where your data resides. If you choose to export/share files, you are responsible for any transfer you initiate.

## 12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version with a new effective date.
